Morgan Wallen

Album: Dangerous

Song: More Than My Hometown

Rank: 5/5
             

With a mix of clean guitar sounds that suggest movement, “More Than My Hometown” depicts a crossroads for two young people. He’s a small-town guy at heart and they’ve known each other forever, but she’s got dreams that can only be realized in a bigger city. The Song takes the listener through this heartbreak.
